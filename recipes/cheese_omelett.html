Cheese Omelette

Description

The cheese omelette is a traditional dish of my kitchen when I don't feel like making anything complicated for breakfast. It usually consists of three eggs with some cheddar, american, and whatever other cheese I can find laying around. Sometimes if I'm feeling really fancy I'll season it with a little salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheeses, preferably a few
  • Maybe some spices if you want
  • Probably butter

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl violently. Mix violently.
  2. Subject the eggs to high heat. Do so without remorse.
  3. Put the cheeses onto the egg mixture. Watch on until they bubble, boil, and melt.
  4. Eat your creation, and imagine the lives those eggs could have gone on to produce...
