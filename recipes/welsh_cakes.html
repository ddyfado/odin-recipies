Welsh Cakes

Description

Welsh cakes are a traditional recipe of the Welsh people. Traditionally served with tea, these tasty baked treats can be enjoyed anytime. They make a great offering for guests or gift to friends or family.

Ingredients

  • a good bit of flour
  • several lumps of butter
  • one small mound of sugar
  • two swigs of milk
  • one nut's worth of meg
  • human intuition

Steps

  1. Put a decent bit of the flour in a large mixing bowl. Add to it nutmeg and sugar.
  2. Drop your milk into the bowl. Mix until smooth.
  3. Fold butter into mixture.
  4. Flour the living daylights out of a clean surface. Flour the living daylights out of your hands.
  5. Pick up your very sticky dough with your flowered hands. Roll out your very sticky dough on your flowered surface using a flowered rolling pin.
  6. Cut you dough into fun shapes like dinosaur outlines or the letters of your name.
  7. Cook until the bottom looks yummy. Flip cookies and repeat.
